## Design Context

### Users
Developers and power users who manage links for campaigns, sharing, or tracking. Used during work hours at a desk — dark environment is appropriate. Primary job: shorten a URL quickly, then track clicks.

### Brand Personality
Sharp, composed, immediate. Not playful, not corporate.

### Aesthetic Direction
Clean & Refined, Dark theme. Warm gold accent (oklch 0.79 0.14 72) — not cyan/purple. Split-panel auth layout. List-based URL cards with border separation, not shadow cards. Typography-led hierarchy.

### Fonts
- Display/headings: Bricolage Grotesque (Google Fonts)
- Body: Manrope

### Color Palette (OKLCH)
- Background `--canvas`: oklch(0.09 0.006 260)
- Surface `--surface`: oklch(0.135 0.006 260)
- Raised `--raised`: oklch(0.18 0.007 260)
- Edge `--edge`: oklch(0.23 0.006 260)
- Text primary `--ink`: oklch(0.93 0.004 260)
- Text secondary `--ink-lo`: oklch(0.62 0.008 260)
- Accent gold `--gold`: oklch(0.79 0.14 72)
- Danger `--danger`: oklch(0.65 0.18 25)

### Design Principles
1. Typography carries hierarchy — no decorative icons needed
2. Gold accent used only for primary CTA and active short links
3. Spacing varies by importance — generous on headings, tight on data
4. Borders (not shadows) to separate content
5. Labels in small uppercase tracking-widest
